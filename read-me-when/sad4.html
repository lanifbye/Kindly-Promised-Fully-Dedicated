Read Me When You're Sad

I know things feel heavy right now. I made this message for moments like this— when the world feels too much and you need a reminder that you're not alone.

Take a deep breath. Let yourself feel everything. You don’t have to rush through it. I’m here, even if just through words on a screen.

You are loved. You are strong. And this sadness will pass, even if it doesn’t feel like it yet.

Whenever you're ready, you can move to the next message or return to the mood list.
