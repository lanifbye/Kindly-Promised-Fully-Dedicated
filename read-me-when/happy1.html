Read Me When You're Happy

Every bit of happiness you're experiencing right now is something you deserve.
Keep whatever brought you joy close at hand.
Enjoy it to the fullest, guilt-free and without hesitation.
After much waiting and hard labor, it is now yours.
Breathe in this moment instead of hurrying through it.
Even if no one else is there, let your heart sing.
Not only did you deserve this today, but you also deserved it for all the silent struggles you kept to yourself.
Even if the joy wanes, keep in mind how it felt. I hope it lasts.
You are free to be soft, proud, and independent.
When you're smiling, the world is a better place.
So go ahead and enjoy yourself without looking back.
