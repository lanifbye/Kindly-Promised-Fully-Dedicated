Dear lovey, This is day 1 of writing for you everyday until your 19th birthday. I'm crafting this message as your hash. Hindi ko papatayin sina hash, mark, dodongs, and so on—pero I'll bury them soon. I don't miss you anymore, hindi na ako natutulala whenever I hear your name somewhere, hindi na kumikirot yung dibdib ko tuwing tinatanong ka sa akin ng family ko—pero I still look back at the old and silly me when we were still close, not together but CLOSE. We became friends muna diba?? Sayo ko namana pagiging chismoso ko, fr. Hindi naman ako marunong mag gossip dati HAHAHHAHAHA. I'm laughing while writing this, those gold old days are flashing back. Lovey, I can reminisce without crying na po. Are you proud of me?? I hope so. I'll make you proud until my last breath. Hindi na kita iniistalk ever since I deleted our last way to communicate. I don't know if you're okay or not pero kung saan ka man diyan, LET IT OUT. Masaya ka?? CELEBRATE AND ENJOY. YOU DESERVE IT, LOVEY. Malungkot, not in the mood, walang maramdaman, or any negative feelings?? IT'S TOTALLY FINE. Cry if you must. You don't need/have to be strong all the time. Lovey, I never saw you as a burden kundi sun. Nagbibigay sa akin ng warmth tuwing nilalamig ako, light kapag madilim, and way para mapalapit ako kay God. You always had this charm which made me love you deeply. If you can't pray for yourself, don't you worry—walang araw na hindi ko yun ginagawa for you simula nung nakilala kita. I'm still bad at words po so please, bear with me:>>I hope you don't mind if cinecelebrate ko parin yung monthsary natin, sa September na po yung last:>> It's funny cause before we broke up, I prayed to God to take away your pain then ayun—he removed me. No regrets tho. Lovey, hindi ko matatanggap hangga't hindi ko pa napupunan yung promise ko. So let me do this as my way of goodbye, I promised you na magpapaalam ako kapag umalis na diba, irog ko? Kahit nakangiti ako habang tinatype to, something feels heavy. Hindi ko alam kung ano at saan pero alam kong meron. Siguro kunwari nag eemote ako kasi Falling For You by The 1975 yung pinapatugtog ko rn HAHAHHAHAHAHAHHAHA. I still love you—but not as my girlfriend or wifey anymore. I see you as my sibling na since parehas tayong anak ni God. Kung puro God man ang sasabihin ko sa mga letters, it's because siya na ang top priority ko. My father, salvation, savior, and only Lord. Well, see you again sa next letter ko hehe.
Your past,
