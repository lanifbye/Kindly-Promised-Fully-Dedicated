Still Yours Somehow

I never stopped thinking of you.
Some nights, I replay your voice.
Time didn’t erase you.
In my silence, you echo.
Love like ours doesn’t vanish.
LDR, but you were always close.
Charise, you’re still my calm.
Across the miles, I still care.
Remembering you is my habit.
Even now, I’m yours in pieces.
