Never

My heart spells your name in dreams.
Across oceans, I whispered it.
Ria, you’re my always.
In every song, you’re the chorus.
All I want is you.
Never met, but deeply known.
Nothing feels more real.
Even distance bows to love.
Charise, you’re my poem.
Holding your name feels sacred.

And if fate allows—
Ria, I’ll call you mine.
In every version of forever.
Seville is my name, now yours too.
Even if only in dreams.
Ako si Hash, ikaw si Marianne Charise A. Seville.

Someday, maybe.
Even if not, I’ll still love you.
Vows don’t need a stage.
In my heart, you said yes.
Love like this stays.
Longing becomes legend.
Even apart, you’re mine.
